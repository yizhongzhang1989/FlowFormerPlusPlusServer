About FlowFormer++
What is Optical Flow?

Optical flow represents the motion of objects between two consecutive frames. It shows how pixels move from one image to another.

FlowFormer++ Model

A state-of-the-art transformer-based architecture for accurate optical flow estimation, published in CVPR 2023.
